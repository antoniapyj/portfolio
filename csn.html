Creative Student Network

Animation|logo|Graphic design

Creative Student Network (CSN) is a project that is designed for students sharing their work and skills, and learn from other students. My responsibility was to design the logo and icons for the website, and animate two short videos for instruction and explanation of the website functions.

Logo & Icons

The client wanted something simple and fun, so then we decided to go with the handdrawn style.

Animation

A short animation film to help the users to understand the website and how to use different functions on the website.
