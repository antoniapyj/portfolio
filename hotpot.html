My first ever completed sketchbook.

Hotpot

Collage

Hot pot, as known as steamboat, a simmering metal pot of stock in the centre of the table. Many ingredients are placed around it and cooked at the table, serve with dipping sauce. I was born and raised in China. Grew up in a big family, hot pot has always been something more than just a meal to me. Nothing feels more heartwarming and happier than sitting at a round table, surrounded by your most-loved ones. Chat, eat and laugh.

For this piece of art work, I used paper materials from old fashion magazines. Finding patterns and colours that matches the appearence of the food and put them together was a lot of fun.
