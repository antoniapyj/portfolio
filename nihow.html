Get to know China like a local does.

Ni How

Web Design|Typeface

A website is designed to be a guide for people who wants to know different part of China like a local. It introduces different culture in different areas of China, provides visiting tips and also teaches different dialects. My responsibilities were to design the website and the typeface.

你, pronounced as 'nǐ', means you. Hello in Chinese is 你好，which pronounced as nǐ hǎo, and that's what 'Ni How' is based on.
