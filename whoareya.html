Raise awareness of racism in football, to recognise recent incidents in football, and to encourage people to challenge racism.

WHO ARE YA: Awareness Raising Campaign

Animation|Web Design & Develop|Graphic Deisgn

Football can unite people like nothing else. However, the beautiful game isn’t free from imperfections and racism continues to happen on and off the pitch. This project was made for Kick It Out and it was shown at Kick It Out live event, recognising its 25th anniversary.

Website & Animation

The website presents the animation and case studies of racist incidents in recent football history. The design of the website matches the style of hand-drawn animation. The colour is bold and high contrast, which attracts the audience’s attention.

Visit here

Posters

A set of posters have been designed to be displayed at football stadiums, to draw football fans who attend the matches to the website and use hashtag #projectwhoareya on social media platforms.
