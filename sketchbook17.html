My first ever completed sketchbook.

Sketchbook 2017-18

Illustration

Although I have learned basic art skills when I attended art high school in China, I would still describe myself as a self-taught artist, or an illustrator. Since I went to study media in university, drawing was more like a hobyy to me. In recent years, my passion for doing illustrations and art got bigger, and I decided to do a challenge to complete a sketchbook, which I have never done before. I chose Moleskine pocket sketchbook, which is about or smaller than a6 size. I am so proud of myself not just to finish this sketchbook, but also trying different mediums such as watercolour and markers. Sketchbook 2018-19 in the making.
